At Your Service

We Post

We post you out a kit in a prepaid postage box.

You Imprint

You get your animals pawprint using the tile.

You Return

Send us back the kit in the prepaid envelope.

We Make!

We'll make a beautiful tile of your pawprint!



After you have placed your order, you will receive one of our special kits within a week. It contains:

  • A piece of special dough to take your pets paw print impressions with,
  • Full instructions on how to use it and where to send it afterwards (also available here) and
  • An order form so you can customise your tile.
The order form shows all the colour options that we have here on the website, so just tick the boxes under your chose options and send it back with your imprint mould. The kit contains a special art dough to get the imprint of your animals paw, instructions on how to take the imprint and an order form for you to choose the colours and text for your tile.
